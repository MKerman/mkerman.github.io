Event Calendar

Third Thursday

Need a date night activity? A night out with the girls?

Visit the factory on the Third Thursday of the month for our monthly Make it - Take it experience. You will get a factory tour and the opportunity to hand dip-dye a candle to take home with you.

Limited Seats. Advance ticket purchase recommended.


Second Sunday, for the Family

The factory is open for group tours and candlemaking demonstrations on the second Sunday of the month. We debut our new seasonal, limited edition candles in October, February, and July. You will have the opportunity to be one of the first to buy the new limited edition candle and see how our new molds are 3D printed.

Group rates are available. Advanced ticket purchase recommended.
