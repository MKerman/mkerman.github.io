Meet the Team:

Arthur Shoemaker

CEO/Founder

During a family trip to Safed, Israel, Arthur was introduced to the art of candlemaking. At University, he studied business administration and fine art. He has blended his love of traditional candlemaking techniques with a modern flair using 3D-printed molds.

Jennifer Norwich

Chief Opperating Officer

Jennifer manages the day-to-day operations of 21st Century Candle Co. She has an MBA from Alphaxo University and a BBA from Bravodine College. She has 15 years of management experience at Fortune 500 companies.

Mark Stemplotwitz

Marketing & Client Relations

As an advertising guru, Mark has brought a new take to how 21st Century Candle Co. markets its products. He has also expanded operations to include customized products for corporate clients. Most recently, he launched a new make-and-take candlemaking experience for the local Pittsburgh area.

George Standlebrook

Director of Candlemaking

George learned the art of candlemaking during his apprenticeship in Safed, Israel. With founder Arthur Shoemaker, George has developed the 21st Century Candle Co.'s unique process of blending traditional candlemaking techniques with modern technology.
